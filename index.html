Welcome to Gresswold Senior School

Meet Our Executive Committee

Our school is home to a team of dedicated and caring educators who are passionate about helping our learners grow and succeed. They use creative and engaging teaching methods to make learning enjoyable and meaningful for every student. Understanding that each learner is unique, they provide personalized support and encouragement, helping students overcome challenges and celebrate their achievements. Our teachers are always looking for ways to improve and learn, ensuring they create a supportive and inspiring environment where every learner can reach their full potential.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in life. We offer a range of subjects and extracurricular activities that cater to the diverse interests and abilities of our learners.

Core Subjects

English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.

Arts and Culture

Drama, Music and Arts and Culture Programs.

Sports & Athletics

We offer various sports such as netball, football, and basketball to support fitness.
